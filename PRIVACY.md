# Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 18, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Types ("we", "us", or "our") handles your personal information when you use our application.

## Information Collection and Use

**Types does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information.**

This application is a utility for managing file types and associations on your local Windows system. All operations, including reading and writing to the Windows Registry, are performed locally on your device. We do not have access to your file system data or registry entries other than what is displayed to you within the application for the purpose of editing.

## Internet Access

The application functions entirely offline. It does not connect to any remote servers to send usage data, analytics, or personal information.

## Data Storage

All settings and configurations are stored locally on your device.

## Third-Party Services

This application is built using Microsoft Windows App SDK. It may be subject to the underlying operating system's diagnostic data collection settings, which are governed by Microsoft's Privacy Statement. We do not integrate any additional third-party analytics or advertising SDKs.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

## Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via our GitHub repository issues page.
